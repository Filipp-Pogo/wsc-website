## SELECTED: Idea 2 — Dark Field + Performance (Option 4B)

This is the direction the client chose after three rounds of iteration. The design uses a near-black hero and navigation with warm parchment (#EAE3D6) body sections. The performance accent is a sharp field green (#7FAD47) used exclusively on numbers, metrics, and primary CTAs. The body sections alternate between parchment and a slightly darker mid-tone. The footer returns to the dark palette. Typography is Inter at weight 300 for headlines, 400 for body, 500 for nav/labels.
